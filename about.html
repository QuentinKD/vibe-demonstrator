Our story

After more than 8 months of preparation, in January, Vibe 1.5 has been uploaded to Scratch's website !

This version is an intermediary version of our user interface. It has been a half success for the company but a very enriching experience.

In september of 2021, Vibe 3.0 is officially released. It is the most advanced graphical user interface ever made with Scratch.

Every single feature you can imagine has been implemented.

So that th user experience is maximised. We hope that you'll like it !
